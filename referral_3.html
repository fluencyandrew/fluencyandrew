A few common questions

Is there a minimum number of referrals expected?+
No minimum at all. Refer when it genuinely makes sense for your clients. A well-matched referral is worth far more than volume, and I'd rather build a long-term relationship with a small number of trusted partners than manage a large, impersonal network.
What if a referred client doesn't sign up immediately?+
Attribution is held for 90 days. If your referral books a discovery call and enrols within that window, your commission is triggered regardless of timing.
Do I need to pitch or sell on your behalf?+
Not at all. A warm introduction or a shared link is sufficient. I'll take it from there via the discovery call — and I'll make sure the experience reflects well on you.
What if I have a corporate client interested in group training?+
Get in touch directly and we'll discuss it. I handle corporate arrangements individually, and commission still applies.
Can you provide materials I can share with clients?+
Yes. Once we've connected I'll send a one-page programme overview you can share, along with anything else that would help your clients understand what they'd be signing up for.
