// How to Join

🎥 How to use Google Meet

Never used Google Meet before? Here's all you need to know — it takes about 30 seconds to get set up.

  • 01Click the Google Meet link in your confirmation email — or find it in your calendar invite.
  • 02If prompted, sign into a Google account (Gmail). If you don't have one, you can join as a guest.
  • 03Allow access to your camera and microphone when the browser asks — this is required to join.
  • 04Click Join now. You'll enter a waiting room — I'll admit you as soon as I'm ready.
  • 05For best quality, use headphones and a stable wifi connection. A laptop or desktop works better than a phone.
