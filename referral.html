A few common questions

Is there a minimum number of referrals expected?+
No. Refer when it genuinely makes sense for your clients. I'd rather receive one well-matched referral than several who aren't a good fit. This isn't a volume arrangement — it's a relationship.
What if a referred client doesn't sign up immediately?+
Attribution is held for 90 days. If your referral books a discovery call and enrols within 90 days, the commission is yours regardless of timing.
Do I need to pitch or sell on your behalf?+
Not at all. A warm introduction or a shared link is all that's needed. I'll take it from there via the discovery call.
What if I have a corporate client interested in group training?+
Get in touch directly and we can discuss it. I handle these on a case by case basis, and commission still applies.
Can I see the programme details to share with clients?+
Yes — once we've connected I'll send you a one-page programme overview you can share, along with any other information that would be helpful for your clients.
